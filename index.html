Sunaulo Bikalpa: Golden Alternative

About

Sunaulo Bikalpa

We are an NGO located in Kapan, Kathmandu. We were established at 2066 B.S. with the aim to help the local community. We have organised several events in Kapan and Budhanilkantha area to raise awareness regarding female health issues and also organized health campaigns to senior citizens. We are also involved in organizing community cleaning activities and spreading employment awareness to youths in the society. We are also actively involved in distributing necessary relief materials in places affected by earthquake, flood or Covid-19.

Vision

To make Kapan a well educated, problem free, clean and healthy society.

Mission

To provide assistance to women,children, elderly and underpriviledged.
